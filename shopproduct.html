White Oud

Product Detail

White oud is a perfume essence that's sweet and spicy, with top notes of artemisia lemon, heart notes of freesias, and base notes of amber and musk. It's also known as agarwood and comes from a rare tree that grows in India and southern Asia. The tree must be at least 50 years old before it produces white oud, which is found in the heartwood. The resin is extracted from the tree through a process called tapping, and then distilled to produce the essential oil.
